Million Dollar Bacon

Description

This bacon is a sweet and spicy treat that will elevate your breakfast to a whole new level. It's perfect for brunch or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound thick-cut bacon
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top.
  3. Arrange the bacon slices in a single layer on the wire rack.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, cayenne pepper, and black pepper.
  5. Sprinkle the sugar mixture evenly over the bacon slices.
  6. Drizzle the maple syrup over the bacon.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and caramelized.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
