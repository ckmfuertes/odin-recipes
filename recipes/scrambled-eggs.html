Boursin Scrambled Eggs

Description

These creamy scrambled eggs are elevated with the addition of Boursin cheese, making for a rich and flavorful breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Boursin cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well combined.
  2. Add the Boursin cheese, salt, and pepper to the eggs and mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  4. Once the butter is melted, pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula until they are softly scrambled and creamy.
  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
